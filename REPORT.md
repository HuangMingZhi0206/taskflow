# INTRODUCTION

This Taskflow application is designed to help the users manage their tasks. We often see that people sometimes forget about the assignments given by their teachers or lecturers, therefore we made this to assist the users in managing the tasks they receive.



# FUNCTION
1.Gather All Tasks: Record all assignments and deadlines in one place.

2.Set Priorities: Mark which tasks are the most important and urgent.

3.Get Reminders: Receive notifications before assignment deadlines.

4.Stay Focused & Disciplined: Helps you stay disciplined and avoid procrastination.

5.Track Progress: See your task progress, from "in progress" to "completed."

6.Schedule Integration: Sync deadlines and reminders with your personal schedule so you can manage all your schedules in one place.
