## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips for Students

### 1. **Use Estimated Hours Wisely**
```
Small Assignment: 2-3 hours
Essay/Paper: 5-8 hours
Exam Study: 10-15 hours
Major Project: 20+ hours
```

### 2. **Set Buffer Time**
Don't set your deadline as the actual due date!
- **Actual Due Date**: Friday 11:59 PM
- **Your Deadline**: Thursday 6:00 PM
- **Buffer**: 30 hours for emergencies

### 3. **Break Down Large Tasks**
Instead of:
- âŒ "Complete Research Project"

Do this:
- âœ… "Research Project - Find 5 sources"
- âœ… "Research Project - Write outline"
- âœ… "Research Project - Write introduction"
- âœ… "Research Project - Write body paragraphs"
- âœ… "Research Project - Edit and proofread"

### 4. **Use Priority Strategically**

**ğŸ”´ Urgent:**
- Due in 1-3 days
- High grade weight
- Exams coming up

**ğŸŸ¡ Medium:**
- Due in 4-7 days
- Regular assignments
- Most of your work

**ğŸŸ¢ Low:**
- Due in 8+ days
- Optional readings
- Long-term prep

### 5. **Check TaskFlow Daily**
Make it a habit:
- **Morning**: Check today's tasks
- **Evening**: Update progress, mark complete
- **Sunday**: Plan the week ahead

## ğŸ† Student Success Tips

### Time Management
1. **Plan Sunday evening** - Review upcoming week
2. **Time block** - Assign specific times to tasks
3. **Pomodoro technique** - 25 min work, 5 min break

2. **Start with easy tasks** - Build momentum
